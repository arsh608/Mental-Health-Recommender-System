ℹ️ About MindPath

AI-powered mental health content recommender system

Project Details

Course: CS4053 Recommender Systems

University: National University of Computer & Emerging Sciences (FAST)

Instructor: Sir Syed Zain-Ul-Hassan

Team: Hafsa Rashid (23K-0064) · Adina Faraz (23K-0008) · Syeda Sara Ali (23K-0070) · Arsh (23K-0078)

Features

  • Natural language mood detection → personalised recommendations
  • 4-layer NLP ensemble (VADER, TextBlob, TF-IDF + LogReg, SentBERT)
  • 85 curated mental health resources (articles, videos, exercises)
  • NLP Explorer with per-layer score transparency
  • Real-time session analytics and mood tracking
  • Weighted ranking: cosine similarity + mood tags + type preference + difficulty
